# Privacy Policy — Orizon

Last updated: 2026-07-05

Orizon is a booru client app. This policy explains what happens to your
data when you use it.

## What Orizon does not do

- No account or sign-up is required.
- No analytics, tracking, or crash-reporting SDKs are included.
- No advertising.
- Orizon does not run its own backend server and does not collect,
  store, or transmit your data to us or to any third party on our
  behalf.

## What is stored, and where

Everything Orizon stores lives locally on your device (local database
and app preferences): the sources you add, your favorites, your tag
blacklist, your download history, and your app settings (theme,
language, grid style, and the adult-content toggle). Uninstalling the
app removes this data.

If you add optional credentials (username or API key) for a source,
those are stored locally on your device only and are sent only to that
source's own server as part of your requests to it — never to us.

## Network requests

When you search or browse, Orizon sends requests directly from your
device to the booru sources you configured (for example Safebooru, or
any custom source you add). Those requests go straight to the
third party site's servers — Orizon does not proxy or see this
traffic. Standard web request data (such as your IP address) is
visible to that third-party site subject to its own privacy policy,
which Orizon does not control.

## Downloads

When you save an image or video, it is written directly to your
device's gallery. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

## External links

Settings includes links to our Discord server and to Buy Me a Coffee.
Opening them is subject to Discord's and Buy Me a Coffee's own privacy
policies, not this one.

## Adult content setting

The adult-content toggle in Settings is off by default. Enabling it is
your own choice and is subject to your local legal age requirements.
Orizon does not verify age and does not promote adult content; what
appears depends only on this setting and the sources you add.
Regardless of this setting, Orizon never displays content that
sexualizes minors.

## Children

Orizon is not directed at children.
